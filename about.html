About *Through the Cracks*

A quarterly magazine dedicated to exposing systemic failures in mental health and substance use care—and advocating for change.

What is *Through the Cracks*?

*Through the Cracks* is an independent magazine that challenges the **fragmented, failing systems** meant to support individuals with **co-existing mental health and substance use challenges (CEMS)**. Our goal is to **bridge the gap between research, frontline experience, and policy reform**, ensuring that real-world stories and evidence-based solutions shape the future of care.

Why It Matters

The system is full of contradictions. People struggling with both mental health and substance use are often told:

  • “We can’t help you while you’re still using.”
  • “You need stable accommodation first.”
  • “Your mental health is too complex for this service.”
  • “You don’t meet the criteria.”

These **bureaucratic failures create a cycle of exclusion**—leaving individuals without the support they desperately need. *Through the Cracks* exists to **call this out, amplify voices from the margins, and push for systemic reform.**
